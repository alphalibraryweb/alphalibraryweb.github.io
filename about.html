The history of Αlphα λibrary

BetaWeb was created on October 7th 2024 by techguy16 as a way to find downloads for beta builds of software, mainly Windows. The first version of the website was released that same day.


On October 23rd 2024, Vix created the δ Catalog, which was commonly known as the Delta Catalog. He announced it on the OpenXP Discord on that day.


On November 8th 2024, the δ Catalog was mysteriously deleted, and later that day, Adrikikicp and techguy16 formed Αlphα λibrary, and rebranded all BetaWeb assets to use that name.
